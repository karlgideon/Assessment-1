Overview

Attack on Titan is set in a world where humanity lives inside massive walled cities to protect themselves from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans. After his hometown is destroyed and his mother is killed by a Titan, Eren Yeager vows to eliminate every Titan and joins the military.

As the story unfolds, dark secrets about the Titans, the walls, and humanity itself are revealed, turning the fight for survival into a complex political and moral conflict.

Key Information

Original Creator: Hajime Isayama

Anime Studios: Wit Studio (Seasons 1–3), MAPPA (Final Season)

Genre: Action, Dark Fantasy, Drama

Original Run: 2013 – 2023

Main Characters

  • Eren Yeager: A determined soldier driven by revenge and freedom.
  • Mikasa Ackerman: Highly skilled fighter devoted to protecting Eren.
  • Armin Arlert: Intelligent strategist and loyal friend.
  • Levi Ackerman: Humanity’s strongest soldier.

Reviews

Attack on Titan has received global acclaim for its intense storytelling, character development, and shocking plot twists.

Critics and fans praise its animation quality, emotional depth, and complex themes about freedom, war, and morality.
